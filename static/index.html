01 Our Findings

By scraping Devpost, discovered that there is a correlation between the number of team members, technology used, and tagline written to the percentage of winning a hackathon.

By scraping Devpost for hackathon submission information, we were able to determine the following facts:




More team members = better chance of winning

We found that as the number of team members increases, the percent chance that their project wins increases. No surprise here!



Top tech to win!

By taking the complement of the intersection of top technologies used by both winners and losers, removing them from our results, and then looking at the top winning technology, we found the following best tech to win a hackathon!

Naive solution = predict all losses

After crunching the data, we found that about 94% of hackathon submissions do not win a prize. The best way to predict whether a project would win or lose with the highest accuracy without any data would therefore be to simply say the project lost! We can do better though.
